About the Project

What is MediQuick?

An online pharmacy and medicine delivery prototype designed to improve how users access medicines and healthcare services digitally.

💊
Online Medicine Access

Enables users to browse, search, and order medicines online with ease.

🚚
Delivery System

Streamlined delivery tracking and scheduling for healthcare products.

🔬
Usability Evaluated

Rigorously tested using Nielsen's heuristics and SUS questionnaire with real respondents.

HCI Principles

Design decisions guided by Human-Computer Interaction principles and best practices.
