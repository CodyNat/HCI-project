Study Overview

You are invited to participate in a usability testing study for the MediQuick - Online Pharmacy & Medicine Delivery System. This prototype aims to improve how users access medicines and healthcare services online. The purpose of this study is to evaluate how easy, efficient and user-friendly the system is when performing basic tasks.

Procedure

If you agree to participate, you will be asked to use the MediQuick system prototype and perform simple tasks such as exploring features or checking available services. After completing the tasks, you will answer a System Usability Scale (SUS) questionnaire based on your experience.

Duration

The entire activity will take approximately 10–20 minutes to complete.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation is voluntary. You may choose not to join or stop at any time without any consequence.

Confidentiality Statement

All your information will be kept private. Your identity will not be shared, and your answers will be used only for academic purposes.

Risks and Benefits

This study has minimal risk, limited only to the time required to complete the activity. There are no direct personal benefits; however, your feedback will help improve the system for future users.

System Usability Scale (SUS)
